Our Vision and Mission

Fashion Nova’s mission is to deliver the latest fashion trends to a global audience quickly. The fashion brand is dedicated to making high-fashion-inspired apparel and accessories accessible to everyone, regardless of their size, shape or budget. By prioritising affordability and diversity, Fashion Nova aims to empower customers to look and feel their best without overspending, subsequently fostering confidence and individuality.

Our Legacy

Fashion Nova was founded in 2006 by CEO Richard Saghian, who identified a gap in the women's apparel market for affordable, stylish clubwear and jeans. Initially starting as a brick-and-mortar store in Southern California, the brand quickly gained popularity and leveraged social media, particularly Instagram, to connect with customers and promote its products. Over the years, Fashion Nova has grown significantly, employing over 600 staff and becoming a major player in the fast fashion industry.
